Main Enemies

Looking Glass Knight

The Looking Glass Knight, also known as the Mirror Knight, was spotted in the Lost Cities in the Far Land Plains. After arriving in the desolated and destroyed city, the conflict began. The Looking Glass Knight's attacks were punishing but in the end was defeated.

Remnant Abyss Watcher Artorias

Sir Artorias, The Abyss Watcher was the forefront Abyss Watcher. Found in the Nether Wastes presumably already corrupted, Artorias engaged in combat. After an intense battle, an advantage was risen and Artorias was defeated, casted away back into the Abyss.

Champion Ignis

Ignis, once a champion of the Nether region. Found at the ruined colloseum, roaming around the once famed location. Ignis was a formidable warrior, but in the end there was no purpose left.

Ancient Ender Guardian

The Ender Guardian, the protector of the End region. Found at the citadel, laying dormant, only to be awoken. The protector fell to the ground as it once stood, defeated by the enemy it once sought to destroy.
